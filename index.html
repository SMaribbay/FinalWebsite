Your Privacy is Not Private

What is Internet Privacy?

Internet Privacy refers to the wide range of technology, systems, and ideas related to giving users more protection when they use the global internet.

Internet privacy comes in many forms that are very common like mandatory privacy statements on websites, data sharing settings, and data transfer initiatves.

Privacy on the internet is important to users because plenty of websites and companies ask for personal information online that could be at risk of being compromised.

Users cannot risk having their personal information being shared online since that information could fall into the wrong hands and be stolen.

What is considered as personal information?

Social Security Number
Home Address
IP Address
Bank Account Information
Usernames & Passwords
